<Section icon={Sparkles} title="New Gameplay Features in the Evolution Test">

The Evolution Test introduces a massive leap in content compared to previous builds, shifting the gameplay loop toward deep emotional bonds and tactical depth. Players explore the starting town of Iia in the Reality Plane, interacting with local merchants and major factions such as the **Nexus Association** and the **True Nexus Syndicate**. 

The primary additions tested during this beta phase focus heavily on how players collect, raise, and fight alongside their companion creatures.

### The Anima Evolution System
For the first time, players can witness the wondrous phenomenon of Anima transformation. Evolving an Anima goes beyond a simple visual change; it alters their combat capabilities, upgrades their skills, and represents the strengthening bond between the companion and the traveler.

| Evolution Stage | Form Example | Gameplay Impact | Visual Scale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stage 1 (Base)** | Inkifoal | High mobility, basic utility skills, small field presence | Compact, companion-style |
| **Stage 2 (Mid)** | Inkicorn | Balanced stats, enhanced elemental synergy, moderate damage | Medium, riding-ready |
| **Stage 3 (Final)** | Atragasus | Ultimate combat passive unlocked, massive battlefield impact | Large, mount-capable |

### Rare Chroma Anima Variants
A major highlight of this beta is the introduction of **Chroma Anima**. These are exceptionally rare color variants of standard creatures that feature unique visual sparkle effects. Encountering them is highly chance-based during overworld exploration, street encounters, or special challenges in the **Apex Club**. 

Once bonded, players can commemorate the encounter by renaming their companion and taking custom photos to share with the community.

<Callout type="success" title="Anima Dispatch Mechanic">
In Iia Village, many shop owners use Anima as cooperative partners. During the beta, you can dispatch your collected Anima to help run local shops, earning you valuable in-game resources and Sciellings (the world's currency) while you are offline.
</Callout>
