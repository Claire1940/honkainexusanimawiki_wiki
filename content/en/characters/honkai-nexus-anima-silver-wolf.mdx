<Section icon={Map} title="Core Character Attributes and Dialogue System">

The progression path in **Honkai Nexus Anima Silver Wolf** is heavily governed by three primary character attributes. These attributes determine how you interact with NPCs, resolve conflicts, and navigate the environmental challenges across the 81 Planes.

Unlike standard RPGs where stats only affect combat numbers, here they dictate your narrative destiny. For instance, having a Charisma score over 7 allows you to charm local merchants for free items, while a low Strength score triggers protective instincts from nearby companions.

| Attribute | Focus | Narrative Effect | Exploration Effect |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Anima Bonding and Nexus Battle Mechanics">

To survive the dangers of the fractured planes, you must bond with mysterious entities known as Animas. The bonding process utilizes Nexus Threads to link your soul with the creature, allowing you to summon them for exploration and deploy them in specialized semi-planes called battlefields.

Nexus battles are highly tactical. Your Animas fight automatically on the field, deploying their unique skills when their energy requirements are met. Your role as the Anima Master is to manage active shields, trigger synergy buffs, and direct focus fire.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to the Cardia System">

The Cardia System is one of the most innovative features in **Honkai Nexus Anima Silver Wolf**. When your fate intersects with another human companion (such as Nana or Kiana), you obtain their Cardia. This represents a deep bond that allows you to transform into them and wield their special combat relics.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish the Fate Intersection">
    Interact with key story companions throughout the 81 Planes. Complete their personal quests and match your dialogue choices to their personality.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Form the Cardia">
    Once the bond is sufficiently deep, a Cardia will crystallize in your heart. You will receive a notification and a unique Cardia Relic.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip the Relic & Select Principles">
    Equip the companion's Cardia Relic in your character menu. Remember that in battle, only the prime Cardia's active Principle will take effect.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Transform and Customize">
    Choose whether to run around as your custom Main Character or transform fully into the companion's form, inheriting their outfits and movement styles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Visual Customization">
Cosmetics are fully interchangeable. Even if you prefer your Main Character's aesthetic, you can still equip companion outfits and mix-and-match footwear to suit your personal style.
</Callout>
