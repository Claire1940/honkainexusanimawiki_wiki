To understand how an Elysia-like figure could fit into the game, we must examine the two primary classifications of powerful entities revealed so far: Novarchs and Anima. Seroplumanth possesses a recorded height of 443.8 units and an energy reading of 196 Joules. The spiritual, nature-aligned characteristics of Anima could easily accommodate a character themed around Elysia's crystal and flower aesthetics.
