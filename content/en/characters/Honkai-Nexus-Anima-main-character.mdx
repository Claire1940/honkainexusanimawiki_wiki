<Section icon={Map} title="Meet the Traveler: The Honkai Nexus Anima Main Character">

The **Honkai Nexus Anima main character** is known simply as the Traveler. As a plane-hopping wanderer, the protagonist begins the story by escaping captivity and arriving in Iia, a quiet town located in the Reality Plane. Unlike traditional heroes, the Traveler’s journey is deeply tied to the broken bonds of the Nexus—paired concepts like Love and Hate or Light and Dark that have shattered across the universe.

As the central figure of the narrative, you do not fight alone. The Traveler's primary ability is to form deep bonds with Anima, which are physical remnants of these shattered conceptual forces. By understanding the Traveler's origin and mechanical role, players can better prepare for the challenges ahead in the Evolution Test.

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Build Your First Anima Team">

As the **Honkai Nexus Anima main character**, your strength in combat relies on the Anima companions you collect and deploy.   <Step num="1" title="Select a Frontline Anchor">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Add Melee or Ranged Pressure">
    Deploy a high-damage companion to pressure the enemy. Scorscythe (War Aspect) provides excellent offensive melee pressure with its bladed tail.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Incorporate Utility or Support">
    Equip a utility-focused companion like Umpurrella or Camcorgi. These companions provide healing, crowd control, or exploration bonuses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Synergize Aspects">

  </Step>
