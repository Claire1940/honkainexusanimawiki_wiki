<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Bai Mei's Role in the Anima Bond System">

While Bai Mei is a human story character rather than a collectible battle unit, her narrative arc is deeply tied to the core Anima collection system. She helps the Traveler understand the nature of Nexus bonds, which represent paired concepts like Love and Hate or Light and Dark. When these conceptual bonds shatter, their remnant power manifests physically as collectible Anima creatures.

Through her quests, players learn how to track, soothe, and bond with various Anima species found throughout the Reality Plane and beyond.

| Revealed Anima | Concept Aspect | Visual Theme |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |
