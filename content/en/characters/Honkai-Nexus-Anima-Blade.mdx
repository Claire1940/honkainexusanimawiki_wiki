<Section icon={Swords} title="Honkai Nexus Anima Blade: Role and Archetype">

In HoYoverse's creature-collector strategy adventure, the character **Blade** stands out as a formidable Eidos. Known as the 22nd Novarch and master of the Plane called "The Peak," he brings intense physical melee pressure to the grid-based arena. Unlike traditional support or utility units, Blade focuses entirely on breaking frontline defenses and drawing aggression away from more fragile backline companions.

**Blade** is officially classified under the Aspect of **Strength**. In the game's tactical combat system, this translates to robust health pools, high stagger values, and self-sustaining mechanics that allow him to thrive when surrounded by enemies.

| Attribute | Base Rating | Combat Application |

| **Primary Aspect** | Strength | Scales damage with maximum health and physical impact |
| **Signature Anima** | Bladehound | Coordinates attacks to trigger flanking damage bonuses |
| **Difficulty** | Moderate | Requires careful positioning to maximize sweep attacks |

<Section icon={Map} title="Lore and the Search for Kafka">

Before the catastrophic sundering of the world, **Blade** served as the sworn guardian of Kafka, the enigmatic 11th Sovereign of "Strength and Weakness". Kafka commanded a dark power great enough to rival Amstradath itself. However, following the Sundering, Kafka vanished without a trace, and her grand faction fell into complete disarray.

Today, Blade rules over the desolate Plane of "The Peak" as the Novarch of Strength. Despite his official duties, he is rarely found resting in his home plane. Rumors spread across the Reality Plane that he endlessly roams the fractured realms, tracking down clues to locate his long-lost Sovereign.

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Build a Team Around Blade">

Success in **Honkai Nexus Anima** relies on building synergistic teams around your anchor Eidos. Because Blade acts as a frontline bruiser, your team composition must provide adequate backline healing, crowd control, or elemental reactions to support his aggressive physical playstyle.

<Steps>
 <Step num="1" title="Secure the Frontline Anchor">
 Deploy Blade in the central front tile to draw initial enemy aggro and activate his passive physical defense bonuses.
 </Step>
 <Step num="2" title="Select a Sustaining Companion">
 Equip a healing or utility Anima, such as Umpurrella or Hoggit, to provide passive health regeneration and clean debuffs.
 </Step>
 <Step num="3" title="Add Backline Ranged Pressure">
 Place high-damage ranged Anima like Scorscythe or Fyrklor behind Blade to pick off targets weakened by his sweeping physical attacks.
 </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Team Placement Warning">
Avoid placing squishy utility companions directly adjacent to Blade. His high threat level attracts AoE (Area of Effect) attacks from elite bosses, which can easily wipe out fragile backline units.
</Callout>

</Section>
