<Section icon={Target} title="How to Build a Team Around Otto">

To maximize the potential of **Honkai Nexus Anima Otto**, players must construct a balanced team that complements his backline strategic capabilities. Because combat in Honkai: Nexus Anima utilizes an auto-battler-style arena system, positioning and role coverage are critical to securing victory.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Deploy a Frontline Protector">
    Place a high-durability Anima like **Fyrklor** in the front row. Fyrklor wields a greatsword and uses Strength-based skills to absorb incoming damage, creating a safe zone for Otto's backline strategy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Melee Pressure">
    Add an aggressive melee threat like **Scorscythe** to pressure the enemy vanguard. Scorscythe's blade-tail attacks deal consistent damage, keeping the enemy from focusing on your support units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Incorporate a Utility Companion">
    Slot in a companion like **Umpurrella** or **Camcorgi** to provide crowd control, healing, or passive stat buffs. Otto's unique skills will amplify these utility effects across the entire party.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Recommended Companion | Role | Aspect | Key Synergy with Otto |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fyrklor** | Frontline Tank | Strength | Absorbs damage, allowing Otto to channel buffs |
