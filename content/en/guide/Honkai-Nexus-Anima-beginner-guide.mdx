### Aspect Synergy and Combat Rules

Every Anima carries the remnant power of an **Aspect** (such as Strength, War, Satiation, or Memory). Matching complementary Aspects on your team triggers powerful passive bonuses.

| Combat Rule | How It Works | Beginner Advice |
|---|---|---|
| **Autonomous Behavior** | Anima attack automatically based on their roles | Focus on positioning rather than micromanaging |
| **Aspect Matching** | Grouping similar or paired Aspects grants stat buffs | Pair Strength with Satiation for high-survivability teams |
| **Chromatic Variants** | Rare color variants have unique passive traits | Keep an eye out for rare variants during exploration |

<Callout type="info" title="Positioning is Key">
Always place defensive units like Fyrklor in the front row. Ranged utility units like Camcorgi should remain in the back row to avoid taking direct melee damage.
</Callout>
