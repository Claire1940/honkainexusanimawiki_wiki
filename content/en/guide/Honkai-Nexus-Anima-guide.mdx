<Section icon={Swords} title="Aspects, Traits, and Team Roles">

Building an effective squad requires a deep understanding of how Anima are categorized. Each companion in your roster is defined by one **Aspect** and one **Trait**. Aspects represent the cosmic concepts the creature embodies, while Traits define their mechanical behavior and tactical role in battle.

### Understanding Cosmic Aspects

Aspects are the foundation of team synergy. When you deploy multiple Anima sharing the same Aspect or paired concepts, your team gains powerful stat bonuses and passive effects.

| Cosmic Aspect | Gameplay Style | Key Representative | Team Role Suitability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Strength** | Physical pressure, high defense | Fyrklor | Frontline tanking and space creation |
| **Memory** | Tactical utility, backline support | Camcorgi | Ranged crowd control and photo utility |
| **Hate** | Ranged magical damage, sleep passives | Mesmerith | Backline main damage dealer (Carry) |
| **Reality** | Balanced stats, physical damage | Cumulon | Mid-row flexible combatant |
| **War** | Aggressive melee pressure, critical strikes | Scorscythe | Frontline or mid-row primary physical threat |

### Key Team Roles for Beginners

To maintain a balanced squad, you should structure your team with clear role divisions. Avoid stacking too many units of the same type, as a balanced frontline and backline setup is much easier to pilot.
