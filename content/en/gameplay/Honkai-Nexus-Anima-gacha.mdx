<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How the Honkai Nexus Anima Gacha Works">

Honkai: Nexus Anima introduces a unique blend of creature collection and tactical auto-battler gameplay, shifting how traditional gacha mechanics operate. Unlike other HoYoverse titles where players pull directly for on-field combatants, the **Honkai Nexus Anima gacha** splits its focus between human leaders (**Kardia**) and collectible battle companions (**Anima**). 

Understanding this split is essential for managing your resources during the Evolution Test (running from July 9 to July 27, 2026) [00基础信息.md].

### The Kardia vs. Anima Split
In combat, your team consists of active Anima units fighting on the field. Human characters, obtained via the Kardia gacha, function as tactical commanders. They do not fight directly; instead, they provide powerful passive buffs, spawn unique battlefield "Laws," and equip "Talisman" gear to maximize your Anima's performance.

| Summon Category | Primary Role in Team | How to Acquire | Duplicate System |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Kardia Characters** | Provide team buffs, Talisman slots, and active Laws | Kardia-limited gacha pools and level milestones | Unlocks advanced passives and boosts buff percentages |
| **Anima Companions** | Active combat units on the battlefield | Aspect Gem shop, Bond Encounters, and event rewards | Evolves the Anima up to 6 times to boost raw stats |

<Callout type="tip" title="Leader Mechanic">
You can carry up to three Kardia characters in a single battle. However, only your designated **Leader** appears in chibi form on the field and activates their exclusive "Laws". The other two characters function solely as stat-boosting support slots.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Summoning Rates, Banners, and Currencies">

The primary currency used for both the gacha system and the direct-purchase shop is **Aspect Gems**. These are earned by completing main story quests, finishing daily missions, clearing Plane challenges, and participating in limited-time events. 

### Gacha Pool Structure
The gacha system features rotating limited-time banners featuring popular cross-over characters (such as Blade from *Honkai: Star Rail*) and highly anticipated original characters like Nanafey and Bai Mei.

| Banner Type | Primary Focus | Base Rate (5-Star) | Pity System Trigger |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Kardia Limited Banner** | Featured 5-Star Character (e.g., Blade) | 1.6% (including soft pity) | Guaranteed 5-Star at 80 summons |
| **Anima Synergy Banner** | High-tier Anima & rare Chromatic variants | 2.5% (higher rate for creatures) | Guaranteed featured Anima at 50 summons |
| **Standard Novarch Pool** | Permanent characters and basic Talismans | 1.2% (standard rates) | Chosen selector at 150 total summons |

<Callout type="warning" title="Duplicate Limits">
Acquiring duplicate Anima from both the gacha and open-world encounters allows you to evolve them. Anima evolution caps out at **6 times**. Any duplicates obtained after reaching the maximum evolution tier are converted into universal shop tokens.
</Callout>
