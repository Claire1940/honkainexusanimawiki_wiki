<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Honkai Nexus Anima Combat and Nexus Battles">

The core gameplay loop of HoYoverse's creature-collector adventure strategy game relies heavily on preparation, tactical positioning, and companion synergy. In **Honkai Nexus Anima combat**, players do not issue direct, turn-by-turn manual commands during active engagements. Instead, combat plays out as an arena-style auto-battler where your collected Anima companions fight semi-autonomously based on their pre-set roles, behaviors, and active bonds.

Success in these Nexus Battles is determined before the timer even starts ticking. Players must analyze enemy lineups, understand the field layout, and position their companions to maximize their natural combat patterns.

| Combat Feature | Mechanic Type | Player Influence |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Arena Battles** | Semi-autonomous | High (Pre-battle placement) |
| **Anima Behavior** | AI-driven combat | Medium (Role-based targeting) |
| **Active Abilities** | Auto-triggered | Low (Cooldown & bond-dependent) |
| **Synergy System** | Aspect-based | High (Team building choices) |

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategy Tip">
Because you cannot manually redirect attacks mid-fight, your opening placement determines whether your fragile backline utility companions survive the initial enemy rush. Always place your high-durability companions in the front row.
</Callout>

### Aspect Synergies and Combat Value

In the lore of Honkai: Nexus Anima, a "Nexus" represents the bond between paired concepts such as Love and Hate, Light and Dark, or Reality and Illusion. In gameplay, Anima carry remnant Aspect power that directly influences their performance when paired with matching or complementary companions.

| Aspect Type | Combat Specialty | Best Synergy Partner | Tactical Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Strength** | High durability, shields | Satiation | Excellent frontline anchor |
| **War** | Melee pressure, execution | Strength | High physical DPS |
| **Satiation** | HP regeneration, buffs | Strength | Sustained team healing |
| **Love** | Crowd control, protection | Memory | Disruption and survival |
| **Memory** | Skill cooldown reduction | Love | Rapid ability cycling |

<Callout type="warning" title="Synergy Warning">
Avoid pairing conflicting Aspects that do not offer shared bonuses. Always check the active synergy tracker on the team-building screen before locking in your line-up for difficult challenges.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Battle Preparation Guide">

To help you secure consistent victories during the 2026 Evolution Test, follow this structured approach to setting up your squad before entering the arena.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Analyze the Enemy Lineup">
    Examine the enemy's composition, looking specifically for high-threat melee attackers or backline crowd-controllers. Identify their Aspect vulnerabilities to determine which of your companions will deal bonus damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Anchor Companion">
    Choose a primary anchor unit to build your strategy around. For a defensive approach, start with a high-durability Strength unit like Fyrklor. For an aggressive approach, anchor with a War Aspect unit like Scorscythe.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Fill the Support and Utility Slots">
    Add partners that cover your anchor's weaknesses. If your main attacker is slow, add a Memory Aspect companion to speed up their skill triggers. If your frontline needs healing, slot in a Satiation companion like Hoggit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Adjust Grid Placement">
    Place your frontline units directly in the path of incoming threats to intercept them. Tuck your fragile support units into the back corners, ensuring they are outside the range of enemy area-of-effect (AoE) skills.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Tip">
If a stage feels too difficult, try shifting your units laterally on the grid. Changing which frontline unit draws the initial enemy aggro can completely alter the flow of the battle.
</Callout>
